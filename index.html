Purpose

A neutral space for useful security conversations.

968Sec is built around the idea that cybersecurity improves when people share what they know, learn from each other, and collaborate responsibly. The aim is to help practitioners, students, educators, researchers, institutions, and industry teams exchange ideas without unnecessary noise.

What we care about

Four pillars, one community.

01

Information sharing

Clear, useful exchange of security knowledge, lessons learned, research, and practical experience.

02

Community building

Connecting people across Oman’s cybersecurity ecosystem so expertise is easier to find and share.

03

Education

Supporting accessible learning for newcomers, students, professionals, and teams improving their skills.

04

International collaboration

Creating links with regional and global communities to exchange perspectives, methods, and opportunities.

The standard

Open by default. Responsible by design.

968Sec encourages curiosity, technical depth, and collaboration while keeping responsible conduct at the centre. The community is for people who want to improve security, support others, and contribute constructively.
