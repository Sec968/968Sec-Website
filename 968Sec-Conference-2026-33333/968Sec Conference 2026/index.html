Not a normal conference.

968Sec Conference is designed for people who want to test ideas, tools, decisions, and operating models under pressure. The format rewards evidence, clarity, and practical execution.

Hamour Tank

Five minutes to prove it.

Start-ups pitch their cybersecurity ideas in a fast, demo-only format. They get five minutes to show the product, then five minutes of direct Q&A from practitioners, buyers, builders, and the audience.

5 minutes: live demo Present the idea by showing it. Product claims, workflows, detections, and outcomes must be demonstrated.
5 minutes: Q&A Questions focus on what breaks, what scales, what is defensible, and what the product actually changes.
No presentation-only pitches Slides can support the demo, but cannot replace it.

Clear rules.

The event is still evolving, but the operating principles are fixed.

Is this a CTF?

No. Some elements may feel competitive, but the core event is about practical cybersecurity decision-making, investigation, recovery, and communication.

Who is it for?

Security practitioners, builders, founders, students, defenders, incident responders, engineers, and decision-makers who want practical exposure rather than passive talks.

Can vendors participate?

Yes. Hamour Tank is designed for start-ups and tool builders willing to demonstrate their product live and answer direct questions.

When are details announced?
